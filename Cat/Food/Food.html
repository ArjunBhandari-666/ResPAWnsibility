Some human foods that are safe for cats to eat are fish, meat, cheese, bananas, berries, melon, carrots, rice, pumpkin, oatmeal, eggs, spinach, vegetables etc. Some human foods that are not safe for cats to eat are chocolate, grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, macadamia nuts, bread, dough, alcohol, Xylitol, coffe, citrus fruits, coconut flesh, coconut water, dairy, nuts, salt etc.
Cats need to be fed a minimum of 2 meals a day with 12 hour gaps between them. A breakfast, lunch and dinner schedule is an equally great option. This way, the cat wont go to long without food. Kittens that are 8 to 12 weeks old require 4 meals a day. kittens 3 to 6 months require 3 meals a day. After that you decide to either give your cat 2 or 3 meals a day. Some dogs may require more meals than that. You will have to consult your veterinarian for your cat.
Most cats need about 50ml of clean water a day for each killogram of body weight they have. For example, a 10 kg cat would need to drink a minimum of 500ml of water everyday.
