Here are some hygiene tips to take care of your cat
1. Brush Their Coats. Brushing your cat’s hair will help remove dirt and dead hair from your cat’s coat and skin. It will also help dispense the natural oils which makes your cat’s coat shiny and gives it that healthy look.
2. Don’t Forget About Their Teeth. How often do you brush your cat’s teeth? Your cat should have their teeth brushed on a daily basis, but even 2 -3 times per week will make a huge difference to their dental health.
3. Clean Their Ears. Cleaning your pets ears will help reduce infection and should be completed at least once a month. Some warm water and a cotton pad will to the trick and they might even enjoy it! For your pets safety we recommend not using a cotton swab.
4. Air Filters are important. Air filters and purifiers can go a long way to removing airborne dirt, dust, and dander from your home environment. They can reduce both odors and allergens and can help your home smell fresher longer, even if you have multiple cats.
5. Nail Trimming is important. We recommend having your cat’s nails trimmed at least once a month, however some cats nails tend to grow quicker than others and may need theirs cut more frequently.
6. Regular Baths. A bath doesn’t have to be just at the groomers once every 6 – 8 weeks. It’s important to have regular baths at home for good hygiene. We recommend washing your cat no more than once in 2 weeks.
