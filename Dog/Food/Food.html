Some human foods that are safe for dogs to eat are carrots, cucumbers, apples, bluberries, bananas, watermelon, green beans, white rice, fish, chicken, pork, turkey etc. Some human foods that harm dogs are avacados, cherries, chocolates, coffee, caffeine, raisins, grapes, nuts, salty foods, raw meat or eggs, onions, garlic, chives, ice cream, coconuts etc.
Dogs need to be fed a minimum of 2 meals a day with 12 hour gaps between them. A breakfast, lunch and dinner schedule is an equally great option. This way, the dog wont go to long without food. Puppies that are 8 to 12 weeks old require 4 meals a day. Puppies 3 to 6 months require 3 meals a day. After that you decide to either give your dog 2 or 3 meals a day. Some dogs may require more meals than that. You will have to consult your veterinarian for your dog.
Most dogs need about 60ml of clean water a day for each killogram of body weight they have. For example, a 10 kg dog would need to drink a minimum of 600ml of water everyday.
